In accordance with the Lambton College Strategic Goal of Accountability, all contracts and proposals that bind the College to provision of services must be authorized by a manager with the authority to do so.
Policy
All contracts that bind the College for delivery of services are to be properly authorized within the limits established below.
The following table identifies the appropriate manager with the authority to bind the College:
President
Contracts over 3 years in duration and $200,000 per annum
or, of a total value over $1,000,000
Vice-President or Executive Dean/Director
Contracts over 3 years in duration and less than$200,000 per annum
or, 3 years or less and of a total value of less than $1,000,000
Director/Dean
of a total value less than $100,000
Manager/Chair
a total value of less than $25,000
All contracts in excess of $25,000 must be reviewed by the Finance Department prior to signing.
The Finance Department sign-off will be by the Director, Finance for amounts between $25,000 and $100,000, and the Senior Vice President, Strategy & Corporate Services for amounts of $100,000 or more.
Exceptions to this policy must be approved by the appropriate Vice-President or Executive Dean/Director.
